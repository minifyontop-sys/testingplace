Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, Bella Napoli has been serving authentic Neapolitan pizza for over 35 years. Our dough is made fresh daily using a traditional recipe passed down through generations.


We source only the finest ingredients from Italy and local farms, ensuring every pizza that leaves our kitchen meets our high standards of quality and taste. Our wood-fired oven reaches temperatures of 900°F, creating the perfect crispy crust with a soft, airy interior.
